🐌 about slugseek

Privacy

Your stuff stays yours. SlugSeek runs entirely in your browser. There's no server, no account, no sign-up, no tracking, and no analytics. We never see the words you type or the domains you check. There's genuinely no "us" on the other end.

To check availability, your browser makes the same kind of lookups any website does: DNS queries to Google and Cloudflare, registration lookups to Verisign's public RDAP service, and word suggestions from Datamuse. Those go straight from your device to those services, not through us.

Your word lists, favorites, and results are saved only in this browser's local storage, on your own machine. Clear your browser data and they're gone. Exporting just hands you a file you control.

Terms, kept human

Availability results come from free public services and are best-effort, not a promise: a name showing as open isn't guaranteed to be registrable (premium and reserved names happen). Always confirm with a registrar before you count on it.

SlugSeek is provided as-is, with no warranties, and you use it at your own risk. That's honestly the whole deal. Happy hunting. 🐌
